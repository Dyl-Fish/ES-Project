General Appearance

The land and appearence of a Temperate rainforest

A temperate rainforest is characterized by its lush vegetation, with vibrant greens and earthy browns blending together to create a vast variety of colors. The dense canopy of the forest provides a home for various plants and animals, each with its own unique appearance. The lush foliage of the forest creates a seemingly endless sea of green, while the underbrush is a mix of ferns, shrubs, and other vegetation. The forest holds tons of movement and sound, as the diverse array of plants and animals go about their daily lives.
