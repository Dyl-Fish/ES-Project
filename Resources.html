The resources in a Temperate Rainforest

Temperate rainforests are abundant in natural resources that are essential for various industries and human activities. Timber is one of the most important resources found here. The dense temperate rainforest provides a suitable habitat for tall, straight trees, making them a valuable source of commercial timber. Additionally, these rainforests are often located near coastal areas, providing an amazing supply of fresh water sources like streams, rivers, and lakes. Rich soil and moderate climate also play a part in creating such useful timber by providing tons of water and materials good for the trees growth.
